This study aims to examine the effect of the Tahfidz Al-Qur’an program on the mastery of Arabic vocabulary among ninth-grade students at SMP Islam Terpadu DOD Medan. The research employed a quantitative approach using a quasi-experimental method. The research design utilized pre-test and post-test measurements. The research instruments were proven to be valid and reliable, and the data demonstrated a normal distribution, allowing for the appropriate application of parametric statistical analysis.
