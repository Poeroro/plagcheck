Kenapa PlagCheck?

Fitur lengkap, harga Rp 0

Setara Turnitin, Quetext, dan Copyscape — tapi open source & self-hosted.

MinHash LSH Detection
Catches exact copy & near-duplicates dalam 0.1 detik per dokumen. 91.7% akurasi.
ONNX Hybrid Encoder
Multilingual MiniLM INT8. Tangkap paraphrase & terjemahan silang bahasa (ID ↔ EN).
Bahasa Indonesia Native
Sastrawi stemming + ID-specific preprocessing. 7K corpus ID (Garuda, Detik, Kompas).
AI Text Detection
RoBERTa + perplexity + stylometry. Detect ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA.
Privasi Terjaga
Per-session cookie. Dokumen lo cuma bisa lo liat. Auto-delete 7 hari.
$0 Selamanya
Open source + self-hosted. Tanpa subscription, tanpa API key, tanpa batasan.
