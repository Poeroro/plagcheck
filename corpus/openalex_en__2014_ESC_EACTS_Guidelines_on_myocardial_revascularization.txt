2014 ESC/EACTS Guidelines on myocardial revascularization

Authors: Stephan Windecker, Stephan Windecker, Philippe Kolh, Fernándo Alfonso, Jean‐Philippe Collet
Year: 2014

The ESC/EACTS Guidelines represent the views of the ESC and of the EACTS and were produced after careful consideration of the scientific and medical knowledge and the evidence available at the time of their dating.
